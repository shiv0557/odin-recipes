Best Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • Banana 1
  • Milk - Whole Milk 10 oz
  • Boost or Bournvita or any chocolate based drink powder

    • Additionally you can use Protien powder in place of any chocolate based drink powder.


Steps to make yummy Banana Smoothie

  1. Add banana and milk and chocolate drink based powder to blender
  2. Blended it for 2 mins
  3. Pour it to cup and enjoy it
